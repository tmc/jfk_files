This document is a memorandum dated May 18, 1961, from the Deputy Director of Security to the Chief of the WE Division, specifically Mr. Calvin W. Hicks, regarding security approval for documentation under the alias "HITCH, Cal William (A)". The memo confirms verbal security approval given to Mr. Richard Major for the issuance of a D.C. driver's license, a Social Security Card, and a AAA Insurance Card. The documentation is intended for "flash purposes" related to an operational assignment in Florida and will not be backstopped. The alias has been registered with RIN/OR. Upon completion of Mr. Hicks' assignment, the documentation is to be returned to TSD/IB. The memo is signed by Victor White.
